About

Randomness is the core of many cryptographic practices today, so having a viable source for it is extremely important.

This service uses a webcam, which sees the near-random nature of rustling trees, and uses that to form these random numbers.
 
In the backend, it queries /random, fetching the seed used to calculate random numbers
